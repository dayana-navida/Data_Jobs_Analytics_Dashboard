# 📊 Data Jobs Market Analytics Dashboard

An interactive **Power BI dashboard** that analyzes the global data job market using about **479K job postings**.  
The dashboard helps understand **job demand, salary trends, required skills, hiring platforms, and job conditions** such as remote work and degree requirements.

---

# Purpose

The goal of this project is to convert raw job posting data into **clear and useful insights about the data job market**.

This dashboard helps answer questions like:

- Which countries have the most data job opportunities?
- Which job roles and skills are most in demand?
- Which roles, countries, and skills offer the highest salaries?
- Which platforms companies use to post jobs?
- What job conditions companies offer (remote work, degree requirements, benefits)?

This project can help **job seekers, recruiters, and companies better understand trends in the data industry**.

---

# Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using:

- **Power BI Desktop** – For building the interactive dashboard  
- **Power Query** – For data cleaning and transformation  
- **DAX** – For creating calculated measures and KPIs  
- **Data Modeling (Star Schema)** – For connecting tables and enabling analysis  
- **Parameters** – Used to dynamically switch between different views such as job roles, countries, and skills  

---

# Data Source

The dataset contains about **479,000 job postings** collected from multiple job platforms.

The dataset includes information such as:

- Job titles and roles  
- Job locations and countries  
- Salary information (yearly and hourly)  
- Skills required for each job  
- Job posting platforms  
- Job conditions such as remote work, degree requirements, and benefits  

---

# Key Dashboard Insights

## Job Market Overview

Key metrics provide a quick summary of the data job market:

- **479K total job postings**
- **4.8 average skills required per job**
- **$113K median yearly salary**
- **$48 median hourly salary**

These KPIs help users quickly understand the **size of the market and compensation levels**.

---

## Job Demand Analysis

This section shows where demand for data professionals is highest.

Using dynamic parameters, the dashboard allows users to explore job demand by:

- Country  
- Job Title  
- Skills  

This helps identify **which locations, roles, and technical skills are most needed in the job market**.

---

## Highest Paying Roles, Countries, and Skills

This section highlights the **highest paying job titles, locations, and technical skills** in the data industry.

It helps users understand:

- Which roles offer the highest salaries  
- Which countries pay the most  
- Which skills are associated with higher compensation  

These insights help professionals focus on **high-value career paths**.

---

## Job Posting Trends

A line chart tracks **how job postings change over time**.

This helps identify **trends and seasonal patterns in hiring across the year**.

---

## Job Platforms

This visualization shows **which platforms companies use most for posting data jobs**.

**Insight:**  
LinkedIn has the **largest share of job postings**, making it the most important platform for job seekers.

---

## Job Conditions

The dashboard also analyzes important job conditions:

- Percentage of **remote jobs**
- Jobs that **do not mention a degree requirement**
- Jobs offering **health insurance**

These insights highlight **changing hiring practices and flexibility in the data job market**.
